Welcome to Village Portal

E-Village: Connecting rural communities through a digital platform, showcasing development initiatives, sharing information, and accessing donations for community growth.

Project Components

The My e-Village project consists of the following key components:

  • Establish a digital platform that connects rural communities, allowing them to share and celebrate their development achievements.
  • Facilitate communication and knowledge exchange among villagers, promoting collaboration and learning from each other's experiences.


Donate Now

Donation to support village development plays a crucial role in uplifting communities and improving their living conditions. By making a donation to a village, you contribute to various aspects such as education, healthcare, infrastructure, clean water supply, and livelihood opportunities. Your donation can help build schools, provide medical supplies, empower local businesses, promote sustainable agriculture, and support community initiatives.

School Activities

Village school activities involve a variety of events and programs aimed at promoting holistic education and engaging the community. These activities may include academic competitions, cultural festivals, sports events, community service projects, educational trips, and extracurricular clubs, all aimed at enhancing students' learning experiences and fostering their overall development.

Gram Panchayat Updates

Gram Panchayat updates provide timely information on infrastructure development, welfare schemes, livelihood and agriculture initiatives, community programs, financial matters, governance, and public grievances. These updates ensure transparency and citizen participation in the functioning of the local government. .
