The My e-Village project

Project Goals

The primary goals of the My e-Village project are to:

  • Establish a digital platform that connects rural communities, allowing them to share and celebrate their development achievements.
  • Facilitate communication and knowledge exchange among villagers, promoting collaboration and learning from each other's experiences.
  • Provide a space where schools and educational institutions can showcase their activities, achievements, and needs, fostering educational development in rural areas.
  • Highlight the initiatives and decisions made by the gram panchayats, ensuring transparency and accountability in local governance
  • Enable the villagers to seek and receive donations for community development from urban cities, NGOs, and government agencies.
  • Promote a sense of pride, ownership, and community engagement by giving rural residents a platform to contribute to the growth and development of their villages
